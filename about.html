About Punjab Land Analytics Portal

Empowering research and policy-making through open access to land data

Our Vision

The Punjab Land Analytics Portal (PLAP) is an initiative of the Punjab Land Records Authority (PLRA) to create a research-oriented platform where historical and current land-related datasets can be accessed in a clean, standardized, and user-friendly way.

Inspired by platforms like IPUMS USA, we aim to democratize access to land data for researchers, policymakers, academics, and the general public while ensuring privacy and data security.

Key Objectives

Open Access

Provide public, controlled access to anonymized land-related datasets for research and analysis.

Interactive Analytics

Offer interactive visualizations including charts, graphs, and heat maps based on PLRA data.

Trend Analysis

Support time-based analysis including trends over years and policy impact assessment.

Privacy Protection

Ensure data privacy, anonymization, and compliance with PLRA policies.

Data Sources

CLRMIS

Computerized Land Record Management Information System - Source of land ownership, parcel data, and mutation records.

E-Registry / ESORS

Electronic System of Registration Services - Source of property registration transactions.

Franchisee System

Service center operations data for operational analytics.

About PLRA

The Punjab Land Records Authority (PLRA) is a statutory body established under the Punjab Land Records Authority Act 2017. PLRA is responsible for maintaining and modernizing land records in Punjab, Pakistan.

With digitization of over 55 million land records across 36 districts, PLRA has transformed land administration in Punjab, making services more accessible and transparent for citizens.
