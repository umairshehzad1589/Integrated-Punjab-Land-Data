Dataset Descriptions

Registry Transactions Dataset

v2.1

Contains anonymized records of property registrations processed through the E-Registry system, including sales (conveyance), gifts, mortgages, leases, and other deed types.

Coverage
  • Time: 2015 - Present
  • Geography: All 36 Punjab Districts
  • Records: ~2.5 Million
  • Update: Weekly (every Sunday)
Source System
  • Primary: E-Registry/ESORS
  • Tables: EStampMain, Purpose, Parties
  • Filter: Completed transactions only
Limitations
  • Transaction values shown as ranges (not exact amounts)
  • No party/owner information included
  • Historical data before 2015 not available digitally
  • Some rural tehsils may have incomplete coverage pre-2018

Land Ownership Patterns Dataset

v1.3

Aggregated statistics on land ownership distribution derived from CLRMIS (Computerized Land Record Management Information System). Includes parcel counts, area statistics, and ownership patterns.

Coverage
  • Time: Latest snapshot
  • Geography: All 36 Punjab Districts
  • Records: ~15 Million (aggregated)
  • Update: Monthly
Key Metrics
  • Khewat (ownership unit) counts
  • Khasra (parcel) counts
  • Total area by land type
  • Irrigation source distribution

LADM Mapping (ISO 19152)

The Land Administration Domain Model (LADM) is the international standard (ISO 19152) for land administration. PLAP datasets can be exported with LADM-compliant terminology to enable international comparisons.

Glossary of Terms

Khewat (کھیوٹ)

A group of land parcels under common ownership. Primary unit of ownership records.

Khatuni (کھتونی)

Sub-unit of Khewat representing possession or cultivation rights.

Khasra (کھسرہ)

Individual plot of land with specific measurements and boundaries.

Mauza (موضع)

Revenue village or settlement - the basic geographic unit.

Tehsil (تحصیل)

Administrative sub-division of a district.

Intiqal (انتقال)

Transfer/mutation of land ownership from one party to another.

Fard (فرد)

Official copy of land ownership record (Record of Rights).

Jamabandi (جمع بندی)

Compiled record of rights, prepared periodically.

Patwari (پٹواری)

Village-level land record officer.

Qanoon Goi (قانون گوئی)

Revenue circle within a tehsil.

Anonymization Rules

Privacy Protection

All personal identifiable information (PII) is removed before data is made available. Re-identification of individuals is not possible from the published datasets.

Fields Removed

  • CNIC (National ID numbers)
  • Owner names and father/spouse names
  • Phone numbers and email addresses
  • Exact street addresses
  • Biometric data
  • Bank account details

Fields Aggregated

  • Transaction values: Converted to ranges (e.g., 1M-5M PKR)
  • Land areas: Converted to size categories
  • Dates: Reduced to month/year (no exact dates)
