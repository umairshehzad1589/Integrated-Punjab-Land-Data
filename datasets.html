Data Quality & Privacy Notice

All datasets are anonymized to protect individual privacy. Personal identifiers (CNIC, names, addresses) are removed before data is made available. Transaction values are shown as ranges. Data is provided "as-is" from source systems and may contain quality variations. Please refer to the documentation for detailed data quality notes.

LADM/ISO 19152 Compliant

All datasets support export in Land Administration Domain Model (LADM) format, compliant with ISO 19152 international standards. This enables interoperability with international land research and comparison with other countries' data.
